Security

Security and trust are built around grounded answers, not fake certainty.

5minBot is designed for public website content. When support exists on the site, the chatbot can answer from it. When support is weak, it can fall back instead of pretending certainty.

Public-site scope

The product is meant for content that already exists on publicly accessible website pages. That keeps the answer layer focused on the material your visitors already use.

Fallback behavior

If the supporting content is missing or unclear, the safer move is a controlled fallback rather than a fabricated answer. This is an intentional product choice.

Why this matters

A premium chatbot experience is not just about styling. It is also about boundaries. Buyers trust products that know what they know and admit what they do not.

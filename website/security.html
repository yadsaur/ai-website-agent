Security

Practical safeguards, clear boundaries, and honest claims

5minbot is built to give teams confidence without overselling security posture. This page reflects the current hosted product as it exists today.

Grounded answers reduce hallucination risk

The chatbot is designed to answer from processed site content and fall back when the answer is not supported.

Source display for supported answers

When the product can cite a real page, it shows the page link so teams and visitors can verify the answer path.

Hosted service architecture

The current hosted deployment runs on managed infrastructure and uses external AI services for embeddings and response generation.

Public content only

The current product is best suited for public websites and public product information, not highly sensitive internal knowledge.

No unsupported certification claims

5minbot does not claim certifications or compliance programs that are not explicitly in place. If your team needs a specific review, ask before rollout.

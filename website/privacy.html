Privacy

A simple privacy posture for a simple product.

5minBot is focused on public website content and a clean testing-to-embed workflow. Privacy decisions should be understandable without digging through noise.

What the product is designed for

The product is designed to process and answer from public website pages so visitors can get help faster. It is not positioned as a generic knowledge bot for arbitrary private data sources.

Why that matters

Keeping the scope clear makes the product easier to reason about. It also makes the buyer experience more honest because expectations stay aligned with how the chatbot is actually trained.

Practical principle

If the content is already intended for your public audience, 5minBot helps that content become easier to access conversationally. That principle guides the privacy posture of the product.

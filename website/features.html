Feature deep dive

Built to answer the questions that decide whether a visitor keeps moving or leaves

5minbot combines content understanding, UI awareness, and grounded answer strategy so your chatbot feels useful from the very first conversation.

Outcome first

It understands the site you already have

5minbot crawls public pages, extracts the content that matters, and preserves heading context so answers stay grounded instead of generic.

  • JavaScript-aware crawling for modern sites.
  • Structured extraction with fallback parsing when a page is messy.
  • Chunking that keeps page title and section context attached.

Conversion-aware responses

Pricing, support, and trust questions get different answer strategies

Not every question should be answered the same way. 5minbot classifies visitor intent and adjusts retrieval plus response style so a pricing question feels different from a trust or support question.

  • Pricing, trust, security, integrations, support, and getting-started intent buckets.
  • Cleaner, shorter answers for high-intent buying moments.
  • Action-oriented next steps when the visitor is ready.

Conversation momentum

Starter prompts and follow-up chips keep visitors from stalling

Instead of opening into a blank chat, 5minbot suggests useful first questions. After each real answer, it can suggest relevant follow-ups that keep the conversation moving.

  • Suggested starter questions on first open.
  • Contextual follow-up prompts after supported answers.
  • Short session memory for cleaner multi-turn context.
→ What does this product do? → Is there a free trial? → How do I get started?

Trust and clarity

Answers stay grounded, and unsupported claims are avoided

When 5minbot has a real supporting page, it can show a source chip. When it does not, it falls back instead of pretending it knows more than the site actually says.

  • Source filtering keeps synthetic chunks out of the citation layer.
  • No-answer responses stay clear and honest.
  • Markdown-rich formatting makes long answers easier to scan.

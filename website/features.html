Website-trained answers

Let the chatbot learn the same pages your visitors use to make decisions

5minBot is strongest when your website already explains the product clearly. It turns those public pages into a live answer surface instead of asking you to rebuild your knowledge base by hand.

  • Reads public pages, including modern rendered websites.
  • Preserves section structure instead of flattening everything into one blob.
  • Best suited for product, pricing, support, and marketing content.
  • Keeps the first rollout fast because the site is already the source of truth.

UI-aware guidance

Help visitors find the next click when text alone is not enough

Some questions are not about product copy. They are about where the pricing link is, where the search bar lives, or where to click to begin. 5minBot can answer those too.

  • Understands navigation, buttons, footer links, and common landmarks.
  • Turns layout knowledge into natural answers like “check the top navigation.”
  • Useful for landing pages, product sites, and multi-section marketing pages.
  • Reduces the “I can’t find it” friction that kills otherwise good intent.

Follow-up prompts

Keep high-intent visitors moving instead of letting the conversation die

Good buyers rarely stop after one question. Starter prompts help them begin, and contextual follow-ups help them continue toward pricing, setup, or plan fit.

  • Prompt chips reduce blank-state friction on first open.
  • Follow-up suggestions feel aligned to the question that was just answered.
  • Supports a more guided experience without feeling pushy or robotic.
  • Useful on pages where visitors arrive cold and need help deciding what to ask first.

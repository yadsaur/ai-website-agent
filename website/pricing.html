Pricing FAQ

What buyers usually want to know before they start

Can I start before payments are fully wired up?

Yes. The current flow is designed to let you process a site, test the assistant, and validate the experience first.

Can I move between plans later?

Yes. Plan changes are designed to be straightforward so you can scale with usage instead of guessing up front.

What counts as a website?

Each distinct root URL you process counts as one website. Pages inside that root are covered by the crawl.

How do I know which plan fits?

Starter fits a single property. Growth fits most active teams. Pro is best when multiple products or white-label rollout matter.

Pricing FAQ

What buyers usually want to know before rolling it out

Can I start with one site first?

Yes. That is the recommended path. Start with your highest-value site, test the experience, then expand later.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Pricing is designed so you can match the plan to how many sites and chats you actually need.

What counts as a website?

Each distinct root URL you process counts as one website. Pages within that root are included in the crawl path.

Which plan is best for a small SaaS team?

Growth is the best default for most small teams because it gives you room to test across multiple flows without forcing a larger rollout.

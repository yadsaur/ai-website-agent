Pricing FAQ

Questions buyers ask before they commit

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. You can spin up your first site in the dashboard and wire payments later while you validate the experience.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Move up when you add more websites or need higher chat capacity, or move down if your footprint shrinks.

What counts as a website?

Each distinct root URL you process in the dashboard counts as one website. Subpages and crawl depth are covered inside that site.

When do analytics unlock?

Growth and Pro include analytics-focused features so you can inspect what buyers ask most and where your site still leaks confidence.

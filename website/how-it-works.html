How it works

From public website to live chatbot in a simple rollout flow

5minBot is designed to keep the first setup easy: train on your site, test the experience, and launch only when it feels ready.

1

Paste your website URL

Start with a public marketing or product site that already explains what you sell, how it works, and where users should go next.

2

Let 5minBot train on the pages

The app crawls the site, extracts useful content, and prepares the chatbot for grounded answers and UI-aware guidance.

3

Test before launch

Use the built-in chat lab to ask pricing, onboarding, and where-to-click questions before the widget goes live.

4

Copy the script tag and publish

Install the embed code on your site and let the chatbot handle the first layer of buyer questions around the clock.

Under the hood

The product work that keeps the experience clear and trustworthy

1

Rendered-site crawl

5minBot reads the real website surface, including JavaScript-rendered pages, so it can work from what visitors actually see.

2

Content and structure extraction

Readable copy, page metadata, and UI elements like navigation, buttons, and search bars are captured as part of the site understanding layer.

3

Semantic chunking and retrieval

Pages are turned into structured chunks so the chatbot can retrieve the right answer quickly instead of relying on generic summarization.

4

Intent-aware reply strategy

Pricing, support, trust, security, and onboarding questions are treated differently so responses feel better aligned to what the visitor actually needs.

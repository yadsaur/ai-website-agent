Terms

Simple terms for using 5minBot

These terms are written to explain the current hosted product plainly. They do not replace custom commercial agreements for teams that need one.

Use the product responsibly

Only process websites you own, control, or are authorized to analyze through the product.

Service availability

5minBot is a hosted software product and may evolve over time as features, plans, and workflows mature.

Content accuracy

The chatbot is grounded in processed site content, but teams remain responsible for the source material they publish on their websites.

Acceptable use

Do not use the product to process unlawful content, abuse third-party sites, or interfere with service stability.

Terms

Use the product for public website chatbot workflows.

The practical spirit of 5minBot is simple: process public site content, test the chatbot honestly, and embed it when the answers are strong enough for real visitors.

Intended use

5minBot is designed for website chatbot use cases where public pages, buyer questions, and clear next-step guidance are the core workflow.

User responsibility

You are responsible for the public content you choose to process, the websites you embed on, and the way you present the chatbot to your visitors.

Service expectations

The product is designed to be helpful, fast, and safe, but it should still be tested before live rollout. That is why the product flow emphasizes review before embed access.
